Privacy Policy

This policy describes how we handle your data when you use our app "Lazy1922", or in Chinese, "懶人實聯制".

1. What do we gather from you?

We gather no information from the end-user.

2. Does our app use secured connections?

Internet connection is only used for in-app-purchase in this app. We use HTTPS for all our connections, so your data is secured.

3. Do we send data to our servers?

All data is stored locally in your device. In fact, we do not have a backend server.

4. Do we encrypt data stored on the device?

All data is stored in plain text within the application directory. It is by your own risk to keep your phone safe from anything that attempts to access data from our app.
